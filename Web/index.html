How safe is your phone?

Take part in a free mobile phone health check to determine how safe your phone really is.

This program tries a number of attacks on your phone to try and detect common security problems.

Mobile Phone Healthcheck

Here's how to scan your phone:

  1. Use your phone to connect to Mobile Phone Health check
  2. Wait a short while as your phone is being scanned for security issues
  3. A report will then be generated showing any potential security problems with your phone

Grilling your phone

I am trying a number of attacks against your phone in order to find any possible security problems.

This can take a little while so just sit tight.
